# CRM TurboWork — Brokerage CRM System

## Role & Scope
Product Designer (UX/UI) • Web • Full redesign • *Status — in development*

---

## Project Overview
TurboWork CRM is a comprehensive CRM system for brokerage firms. The project included a complete redesign of the existing CRM to improve efficiency, user experience and functionality.

The system helps manage customer relationships, optimizes business processes and increases employee productivity. The solution supports sales departments, financial brokers, support service and affiliate partners.
